## How to Use These Badges

1. **Copy the markdown code** from above
2. **Paste at the top of README.md** after the title
3. **Customize URLs** if needed (replace `fivepanelhat/CAT-mail` with your repo path)
4. **Test rendering** on GitHub to ensure they display correctly
